COMMERCIAL GENERAL LIABILITY POLICY
Policy Number: CGL-2024-00142
Insured: Acme Manufacturing Corp
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Expiry Date: December 31, 2025

SECTION I — COVERAGE SUMMARY
This policy provides Commercial General Liability (CGL) coverage on an occurrence
basis. Coverage applies to bodily injury and property damage arising out of the
insured's business operations, products, and completed operations.

Coverage A — Bodily Injury and Property Damage Liability
  Each Occurrence Limit:        $1,000,000
  General Aggregate Limit:      $2,000,000
  Products-Completed Ops Limit: $2,000,000

Coverage B — Personal and Advertising Injury
  Each Person/Organization Limit: $1,000,000

Coverage C — Medical Payments
  Each Person Limit: $10,000

Deductible: $5,000 per occurrence

SECTION II — INSURED PREMISES
The following premises are covered under this policy:
  1. 100 Industrial Parkway, Springfield, IL 62701
  2. 250 Commerce Drive, Warehouse B, Springfield, IL 62702

SECTION III — EXCLUSIONS
This policy does NOT cover:
  a) Expected or intended injury: Bodily injury or property damage expected
     or intended from the standpoint of the insured.
  b) Contractual liability: Liability assumed under any contract or agreement,
     except for incidental contracts.
  c) Liquor liability: Liability arising from the manufacture, distribution,
     sale, or service of alcoholic beverages.
  d) Workers' compensation: Any obligation under workers' compensation,
     disability benefits, or similar laws.
  e) Pollution: Bodily injury or property damage arising from the actual,
     alleged, or threatened discharge of pollutants.
  f) Aircraft/watercraft: Liability arising from the ownership or use of
     aircraft or watercraft.
  g) War and terrorism: Loss arising directly or indirectly from war,
     invasion, or acts of terrorism.
  h) Professional services: Liability arising from the rendering of or
     failure to render professional advice or services.
  i) Intentional acts: Damage caused by deliberate or willful misconduct.

SECTION IV — CONDITIONS
1. Occurrence Basis: Coverage applies to occurrences during the policy period,
   regardless of when the claim is made.
2. Reporting Requirement: All claims must be reported to the insurer within
   30 days of the incident or discovery.
3. Cooperation Clause: The insured must cooperate fully in the investigation,
   defense, or settlement of any claim.
4. Subrogation: The insurer reserves the right to recover payments made under
   this policy from responsible third parties.
5. Premium Audit: The final premium is subject to audit at policy expiration
   based on actual payroll, sales, and other exposure bases.
6. Cross-Liability: Each insured is treated separately, but the total liability
   shall not exceed the policy limits.
