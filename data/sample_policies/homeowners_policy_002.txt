SECTION III — PERILS INSURED AGAINST
This is an HO-3 Special Form policy providing open-perils coverage for
the dwelling and named-perils coverage for personal property.

Named perils for personal property include: fire, lightning, windstorm,
hail, explosion, riot, aircraft, vehicles, smoke, vandalism, theft,
falling objects, weight of ice/snow/sleet, accidental discharge of water,
sudden tearing of heating/cooling systems, freezing of plumbing, and
sudden damage from electrical current.

SECTION IV — EXCLUSIONS
This policy does NOT cover:
  a) Flood: Damage caused by surface water, tidal waves, or overflow of
     bodies of water. (Flood insurance available separately through NFIP.)
  b) Earthquake: Damage caused by earth movement, including aftershocks.
     (Earthquake endorsement available for additional premium.)
  c) Ordinance or law: Increased construction costs required to comply with
     current building codes following a covered loss.
  d) Power failure: Loss resulting from interruption of power off the
     insured premises.
  e) Neglect: Failure of the insured to protect property after a loss.
  f) War: Loss arising from war, insurrection, or nuclear hazard.
  g) Intentional loss: Damage caused by the insured or at the insured's
     direction.
  h) Business pursuits: Liability arising from a business operated in
     the home (home-based business endorsement available).

SECTION V — CONDITIONS
1. Claim Filing: Claims must be filed within 60 days of the date of loss.
2. Proof of Loss: A signed proof of loss must be submitted within 90 days.
3. Vacancy: Coverage is suspended if the dwelling is vacant for more than
   60 consecutive days.
4. Mortgagee Clause: First mortgagee: Pacific Northwest Bank, ISAOA.
5. Cancellation: The insurer may cancel for non-payment with 10 days notice;
   15 days notice for any other reason within the first 60 days; 30 days
   thereafter.
6. Inflation Guard: Coverage A and B automatically increase 4% annually.
